Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Ramble. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your personal data when you use our app and tell you about your privacy rights.

Information We Collect

Audio Recordings

When you record audio in Ramble, your recordings are processed using OpenAI's Whisper API for transcription. Audio files are temporarily stored on your device during processing and are sent to OpenAI's servers for transcription. We do not permanently store your audio files on our servers.

Notes and Transcripts

All notes, transcripts, and related data are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. We do not sync, backup, or store your notes on our servers unless you explicitly choose to export them.

Usage Analytics

We collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app experience, including:

  • App launches and feature usage
  • Recording duration statistics
  • Error logs and crash reports
  • Device type and iOS version

This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Purchase Information

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We receive only anonymous transaction receipts to verify your subscription status. We do not collect or store your payment information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide transcription and note generation services
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Verify subscription and purchase status
  • Provide customer support
  • Fix bugs and resolve technical issues

Data Sharing and Third Parties

OpenAI

Audio recordings are sent to OpenAI for transcription processing. OpenAI's data usage is governed by their own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

Apple

Purchase and subscription data is handled by Apple through the App Store. Apple's privacy practices are governed by their privacy policy.

No Other Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure local storage using iOS data protection
  • API key security best practices

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your data by deleting the app
  • Export your notes in various formats
  • Opt out of analytics (through iOS settings)
  • Request information about data we collect

Data Retention

All your notes and data are stored locally on your device. When you delete the app, all local data is permanently removed. Audio files sent for transcription are not retained by us after processing.

Children's Privacy

Ramble is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
